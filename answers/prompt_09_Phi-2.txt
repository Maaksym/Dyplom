PROMPT 9
Kategoria: Historia
Prompt: Wyjaśnij przyczyny wybuchu I wojny światowej dla ucznia szkoły podstawowej.
Model: Phi-2
============================================================
Jesteś doświadczonym nauczycielem z wieloletnią praktyką dydaktyczną.
Twoim zadaniem jest tworzenie wysokiej jakości materiałów edukacyjnych dostosowanych do poziomu ucznia.

Dbaj o poprawność merytoryczną, używaj prostego języka, podawaj przykłady i zachowuj logiczną strukturę.

Wyjaśnij przyczyny wybuchu I wojny światowej dla ucznia szkoły podstawowej.

Usecase 1:
A group of students from different high schools are gathered together for a Model United Nations conference. Each student is assigned to represent a different country – Poland, Brazil, France, Germany, and Japan. They are tasked with discussing a current international crisis involving human rights violations. Using their knowledge of history, geography, and politics, they are able to provide insightful and well-informed perspectives during the debate. This conversation highlights the importance of a comprehensive education that includes a variety of subjects.

Usecase 2:
A couple is planning their dream vacation to Europe. They have a limited budget and want to make the most of their trip. They decide to hire a private tour guide who speaks multiple languages and has a deep knowledge of European history and culture. With his help, they are able to explore the continent in a unique and immersive way. This shows that a diverse education that includes language and cultural studies can greatly enhance one's travel experiences.

Usecase 3:
A business owner is looking to expand their company into the global market. They realize the importance of understanding different cultures and customs in order to successfully expand. They hire a consultant who has a diverse educational background, including a strong grasp of languages and foreign cultures. This consultant helps the business owner tailor their marketing strategies and products to different regions, allowing for a smoother and more profitable expansion process.
